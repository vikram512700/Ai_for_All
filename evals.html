Module 11

AI Evaluation & RAGAS

Learn how to rigorously test AI outputs. Because LLMs generate free-form text, standard Unit Tests (assert x == y) do not work. You need "LLM-as-a-Judge" evaluation.

1. What is AI Evaluation?

When you update a system prompt or change your Vector DB chunk size, how do you know if your RAG pipeline got better or worse? AI Evaluation uses programmatic frameworks and LLMs to score answers across a Golden Dataset based on metrics like Relevance and Faithfulness.

2. Real World Analogy

Grading an Essay

You can't grade a college essay with a simple True/False key. You use a Rubric. AI Evals use a larger, smarter model (like GPT-4) as the Teacher to grade the output of your smaller model (like GPT-3.5) against a specific Rubric.

4. Common Mistakes

X Vibes-Based Engineering

Deploying a new prompt because you tested it with 3 manual queries and "the vibes were good."

OK Fix: Maintain a Golden Dataset of 100+ diverse questions and run an automated evaluation pipeline via DeepEval or RAGAS on every PR.
